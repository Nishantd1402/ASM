KABBADI

Game Rules

  • Failure to report on time will result in a match forfeit or walkover for the opposing team.
  • • Teams can substitute players during breaks or timeouts.
  • 2. Knockout Format: The tournament will follow a knockout format
  • 3. Match Points and Format: Matches consist of two halves, each lasting 7 minutes.
  • • Overtime: If the match is tied at the end of regulation, there will be a 5-minute extra time period.
  • 4. Raid Rules: The raider must chant "Kabaddi" continuously while raiding.
  • o Each raid lasts a maximum of 30 seconds.
  • o Successful raids earn points based on the number of defenders tagged.
  • 5. Defensive Rules: Defenders must tackle the raider in a fair manner.
  • o Illegal actions like pulling hair or holding the neck are prohibited.
  • 6. Time-Outs: Each team is allowed two time-outs per half, each lasting 30 seconds.
  • 7. Fouls:Stepping out of bounds- If player steps out of the boundary lines during raid, they are declared out.
  • 8. Conduct Rules: The referee’s decision is final.
  • • Rough play and ny abusive or physical misbehavior can lead to disqualification of the entire team.
  • 9. Attire and Instructions: Players must wear proper kabaddi uniforms(no jeans allowed).
  • • Players must not wear any equipment or accessories including jewelry or anything that may cause injury.
