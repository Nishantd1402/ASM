VOLLEY BALL

Game Rules

  • 1: Type of Tournament: Knockout matches.
  • 2: Number of Teams: Tournament will have a total of 8 teams participating distributing in two slots(A,B).
  • • Each team can have up to 6 players on the field.
  • • Teams can have up to 3 substitutes, and rolling substitutions are allowed.
  • • Three-set matches are two sets to 25 points and a third set to 15 points. Each set must be won by two points.
  • • Each team is permitted two 60-second timeouts per set.
  • • Matches will consist of two 12-minute halves, except for the final, which will have two 15-minute halves.
  • • There will be a 3-minute half-time break.
  • • If a match is tied, including the semi-finals, it will go to a best-of-3 penalty shootout.
  • • Any delay by the teams will result in a reduction of match time.
  • 3. Player Conduct: Abusive language, violence, or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in disciplinary action.
  • • Fouls will result in a free-kick or a penalty kick if committed in the penalty area.
  • 4. Goalkeeper Rules: Goalkeepers can use their hands only inside the penalty area.
  • • Goal kicks and throws must be taken from the goalkeeper's box.
  • • No back-passing to the goalkeeper; doing so will result in an indirect free-kick for the other team.
  • 5. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes must be reported in writing to the organizers within 30 minutes after the match.
  • • The organizers' decision on disputes is final.
  • 6. Team Attire: Players must wear proper sports attire, including shin guards, and teams should have matching kits or bibs.
  • 7. Match Officials: Referees will officiate each match, and their decisions are final.
  • • Any changes in the rules will be communicated by the match officials.
  • 8. Applicability of Futsal Rules: All other futsal rules are applicable to this tournament unless specified otherwise.
