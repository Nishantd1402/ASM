Game Rules

  • 1: Tournament Format
  • • 1. The format of the chess event is solo knockouts.
  • • 2. The player who wins would proceed forward and the player who loses gets eliminated.
  • • 3. The time control for the event would be 3+2(blitz).
  • • 4. Everyone must have the Chess Clock app installed on their smartphones.
  • • 5. If a player fails to attend his/her game on time the opponent would be given a bye and would proceed further.
  • • 6. Fide Rules will be final.
  • 2: Rules of Fide
  • • 2 illegals would result in a loss..
  • • 1st illegal would increment the opponent’s time by 2 minutes.
  • 3: Player Conduct
  • • Abusive language, violence, or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in disciplinary action.
  • 4: Reporting to Matches
  • • Late arrivals may result in a forfeit or walkover for the other.
