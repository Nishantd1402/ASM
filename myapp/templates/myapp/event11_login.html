Game Rules

  • Event Overview
  • 1: Format- Knock-out matches.
  • 2: Top 8 Seeding- The top 8 players will receive seeds based on rankings or past performances.
  • 3: Spot Entries- Spot entries are allowed until the start of the event.
  • 4: Match Points- Each game is played to 11 points..
  • 5: Match Format-
  • • All matches up to the semi-finals: Best of 3 games.
  • • Semi-finals: Best of 5 games.
  • • Finals: Best of 7 games.
  • 6: Match Schedule
  • • Players will be informed of their match schedule half an hour before the event start.
  • • Players must be ready to play 5 minutes before their scheduled time.
  • • Failure to report on time will result in a match forfeit or a walkover for the opposing player.
  • 7: Come in proper Sports attire. Jeans, joggers will not be allowed.
  • Note: Refree's decision will be Final Decision.
