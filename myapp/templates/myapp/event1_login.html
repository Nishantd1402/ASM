FOOTBALL

Game Rules

  • 1: Tournament Format: The tournament will have 8 teams, and each match will be knockout style.
  • 2: General Rules: Each team can have up to 6 players on the field.
  • • Teams can have up to 3 substitutes, and rolling substitutions are allowed.
  • • The referee's decision is final.
  • 3: Match Duration: Matches will consist of two 12-minute halves, except for the final, which will have two 15-minute halves.
  • • There will be a 3-minute half-time break.
  • • If a match is tied, including the semi-finals, it will go to a best-of-3 penalty shootout.
  • • If the best-of-3 shootout ends in a tie, the match will go to sudden death, meaning one penalty each until one team misses.
  • • If the final is tied, there will be two 5-minute halves of extra time, followed by a best-of-3 penalty shootout if necessary.
  • • Any delay by the teams will result in a reduction of match time.
  • 4: Player Conduct: A player receiving a yellow card must sit out for 2 minutes.
  • • A player receiving a red card is ejected and cannot be replaced, leaving the team with one less player.
  • • Abusive language, violence, or unsportsmanlike behavior may result in disciplinary action.
  • • Fouls will result in a free-kick or a penalty kick if committed in the penalty area.
  • 5: Goalkeeper Rules: Goalkeepers can use their hands only inside the penalty area.
  • • Goal kicks and throws must be taken from the goalkeeper's box.
  • 6: Throw-ins: Throw-ins must be taken from the ground.
  • 7: Dispute Resolution: Any disputes must be reported in writing to the organizers within 30 minutes after the match.
  • 8: Team Attire: Players must wear proper sports attire, including shin guards, and teams should have matching kits or bibs.
  • • Referees will officiate each match, and their decisions are final.
