Game Rules

  • 1. A team shall consist of 9+3 players (9 playing and 3 substitutes).
  • 2. All the rules should be followed as per kho-kho federation.
  • 3. Aggressive behaviour and foul language can result in the player or even the whole team being disqualified.
  • 4. Team should be at the venue half an hour before the allotted match time, failing to which team may be disqualified.
  • 5. A match consists of two innings with each inning consisting of chasing and running turns of 5 minutes each for boys and 5 minutes each for girls.
  • 6. Semi-final and final will be of 7 minutes for boys and 5 minutes for girls for each inning.
  • NOTE: The above-mentioned rules and regulations are to be strictly followed by the team. If there is any disobedience with regards to the rules and regulations by any team, it may result in disqualification.
