BASKETBALL

Game Rules

  • • Type of Tournament: Knockout matches.
  • • Number of Teams: Tournament will have a total of 8 teams participating distributing in two slots(A,B).
  • • Team Composition:Each team must have a minimum of 7 players & maximum of 14 players.
  • • Match Duration: Each game will consist of two 2 halves with each running half lasting seven (7) minutes in length. During the last minute of the 2nd half, the clock will stop at all dead ball situations.
  • 2. Game Rules: Each team will be allowed one 1 time-out per game. A time-out will be one 1 minute in length.
  • • A jump ball will start each game. Players are allowed to be on either side of the court.
  • • A goal is worth one point and is registered when the ball completely breaks the vertical plane of the goal line.
  • • A throw-in is awarded to the opponent when one team loses the ball out of bounds on a sideline.
  • • Defensive players can use their bodies to obstruct an opponent either with or without the ball.
  • • Uniform: Teams must wear jersey and shorts with shoes compulsory.
  • Disrespectful behaviour/ use of foul language/ any form of physical altercation will lead to warnings, fouls, or ejection from game.
  • Referees: Two referees will be assigned to each game, and their decisions are final.
  • Injuries and Substitutions: Players who sustain injuries must immediately leave the court.
  • Player Ejection: Any player who receives 2 technical fouls will be disqualified for the rest of the game and also for the next game.
  • Uniform Conflict: If player is not in proper uniform then he will be not allowed to play the match.
