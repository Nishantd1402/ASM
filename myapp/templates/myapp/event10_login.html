Game Rules

  • 1.⁠ ⁠Format: Knock-out matches.
  • 2.⁠Top 8 Seeding: The top 8 players will receive seeds based on rankings or past performances.
  • 3. ⁠Match Points: Each game is played to 11 points.
  • 4. Match Format: Match will have 2 format – Singles, Doubles ( both players should be of same department)
  • • All matches up to the semi-finals: 1 set of 11 points.
  • • Semi-finals and Finals: 3 set of 21 points.
  • 5. ⁠Rules: All matches will be conducted according to the standard Badminton World Federation (BWF) rules
  • • Service Rules: All serves must be legal as per BWF rules..
  • • Time-Outs: Each player is allowed one 1-minute time-out per match..
  • 6.Conduct Rules
  • •⁠ Referee’s Decision: The referee’s decision is final in all cases.
  • •⁠ Player Conduct: The use of foul language, unsportsmanlike conduct will result in disciplinary action.
  • 7.Tournament Scheduling.
  • • Match Schedule: Players will be informed of their match schedule half an hour before the event start.
  • • Failure to report on time will result in a match forfeit or a walkover for the opposing player Equipment and Attire
  • •⁠ Equipment: Players must their own badminton rackets..
  • • ⁠⁠Attire: Players must wear proper sports attire (no jeans allowed). Only clean indoor shoes are allowed on the court.
