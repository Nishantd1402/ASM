Game Rules

  • 1.⁠ ⁠Tournament Format: Matches will be played in knockout format.
  • • ⁠The tournament will be played with tennis ball.
  • • Sports shoes are compulsory for every player.
  • • ⁠For women matches number of overs in the match and rules may vary.
  • 2.⁠ ⁠General Rules:
  • • The tournament will follow a 11-a-side format , each team allowed a maximum of 11 players on the field at a time.
  • • ⁠The match is of 5 overs with 1 over allowed for powerplay allowing only 2 fielders outside the circle.
  • • The Semi-finals and Finals will be of 6 overs and 2 overs of powerplay.
  • • Overstepping by the bowler will be a No-ball.
  • • Ball bowled directly above the waist height of batsman will also be a No-ball
  • • More than one bouncer above the shoulder level of batsman in an over will be considered a No-ball.
  • • A bowler is allowed to bowl only 1 over and in Semi-finals and Finals a bowler can bowl a max of 2 overs
  • • ⁠All types of dismissals are allowed.
  • • 1 run will be given to batting team for every wide and No-ball without counting the delivery.
  • 3.⁠ ⁠Dispute Resolution:
  • • Disputes or complaints must be raised to organizing committee in writing within 30 minutes of match's conclusion.
  • •⁠ ⁠If there is confusion between umpire and staff , decision will be given by Umpire and Cricket Event Head.
  • 4.⁠ ⁠Reporting to Matches: Teams must report 15 minutes before the scheduled match time.
  • •⁠ ⁠Failure to report on time will result in a match forfeit or walkover for the opposing team.
