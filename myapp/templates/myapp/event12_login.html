Game Rules

  • TO BE EDITED
  • • Type of Tournament: Knockout/League/Round-robin.
  • • Number of Teams: Tournament will have a total of X teams participating.
  • • Team Composition:
  • • Each team must have a minimum of 5 players and a maximum of 12 players.
  • • Teams should have at least 1 coach or team manager.
  • • Match Duration:
  • • Each game will consist of four quarters, each lasting 10 minutes (adjustable based on the format).
  • • Timeouts: Each team is permitted two 60-second timeouts per half.
  • 2: Game Rules
  • • Playing Time:
  • • Each quarter will last 10 minutes (or 12 minutes for specific formats).
  • • 2-minute breaks between quarters and a 10-minute break at halftime.
  • • In case of a tie, there will be an overtime of 5 minutes.
  • •Scoring:
  • • Field goals inside the three-point arc are worth 2 points.
  • • Field goals beyond the three-point arc are worth 3 points.
  • • Each successful free throw is worth 1 point
  • • Substitutions:
  • • Unlimited substitutions, but only during dead-ball situations or timeouts.
  • • Jump Ball:
  • • The game begins with a jump ball, and subsequent possessions alternate.
  • 3: Player Eligibility
  • • Players must be officially registered with their team before the tournament begins.
  • • No player is allowed to play for more than one team during the tournament.
  • • Teams must wear matching uniforms, with distinct numbers for each player.
  • 4:Fouls and Violations
  • • Personal Fouls:
  • • Each player is allowed up to 5 personal fouls before being disqualified.
  • •Team Fouls:
  • •After a team accumulates 4 team fouls in a quarter, any additional fouls will result in free throws for the opposing team.
  • •Technical Fouls:
  • • Technical fouls for unsportsmanlike conduct will result in 2 free throws for the opposing team and possession of the ball.
  • • Two technical fouls lead to player ejection.
  • •Common Violations:
  • • Traveling, double dribble, and carrying the ball result in a turnover.
  • • Backcourt violation will be enforced after 8 seconds of ball possession in the backcourt.
  • 5. Equipment
  • • Basketball Size:
  • • Size 7 (29.5 inches) for men’s games.
  • • Size 6 (28.5 inches) for women’s games.
  • • Court:
  • • Standard basketball court dimensions (28.65m x 15.24m).
  • • Basket Height:
  • • The basket will be 10 feet from the floor.
  • •Uniform:
  • • Teams must wear jerseys of the same color with clearly visible numbers on the front and back.
  • 6. Code of Conduct
  • • Players and coaches are expected to demonstrate good sportsmanship and respect for referees, officials, and opponents at all times.
  • • Disrespectful behavior, use of foul language, or any form of physical altercation will lead to warnings, technical fouls, or ejection from the game.
  • • Fans and supporters should maintain discipline and avoid interrupting the game.
  • 7. Tie-Breaking Criteria (For League Format)
  • • Head-to-Head: The winner of the game between tied teams will be ranked higher.
  • • Points Difference: If teams are tied, the point differential between points scored and conceded will determine rankings.
  • • Extra Time: In knockout stages, if the match is tied, a 5-minute overtime period will be played. If still tied, another overtime will follow until a winner is determined.
  • 8. Referees and Officials
  • • Referees: Two referees will be assigned to each game, and their decisions are final.
  • • Scorekeeper and Timekeeper: Each game will have an official scorekeeper and timekeeper.
  • 9. Injuries and Substitutions
  • • All other futsal rules are applicable to this tournament unless specified otherwise.
