Game Rules

  • 1.⁠ ⁠Players must have registered through the website of Annual Sports Meet
  • 2.⁠ ⁠Matches will be in knockout format.
  • 3.⁠ ⁠Player should reach at the venue as per the guidelines given in the WhatsApp group..
  • 4.⁠ ⁠There will be only singles for both men and women. No doubles matches will be conducted..
  • 5.⁠ ⁠All standard rules will be followed as per the ICF (international Carrom federation).
  • 6.⁠ ⁠Players get three chances in their first turn to "break".
  • 7.⁠ ⁠In those chances, it does not matter whether the striker hits the carrom men or not..
  • 8.⁠ ⁠When a player pockets the chosen color carrom or queen, they are provided with a second chance.
  • 9.⁠ ⁠Player for break will be decided by refree with a toss.
  • 10.⁠ ⁠The first player who'll have "break" will get the white as chosen color..
  • 11.⁠ ⁠First round will be of 3 boards with a Best of 3 form
