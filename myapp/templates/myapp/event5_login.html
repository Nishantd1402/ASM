Game Rules

  • Event Name:
  • 1.100m dash.
  • 2.4x100 m relay
  • 1.If the participant leaves before the clap he/she will be disqualified.
  • 2.Lanes will be assigned to each participant .If in case any participant changes his/her lane will be disqualified.
  • 3.The tournament rounds will be in the form of heats which will be decided based on the number of participants.
  • 4.Refree's decision will be the final decision
  • 5.⁠⁠In case of Relay batton will be given to each team which should to passed on to the respective teammates.
  • 6.⁠⁠If the batton falls or is passed after the player has crossed the line , that team will be disqualified.
  • 7.⁠ ⁠Sportsmanship: All players must demonstrate good sportsmanship, respect for referees, and fellow participants.
  • 8.⁠ ⁠Prohibited Conduct: Abusive language, inappropriate gestures, or any form of intimidation will not be tolerated.
